QUEM É MICHELLE BERNARDO?

‪Quem me vê não lê. Não de tão longe. Sou escrita com letras pequenas. Bem pequenas. Para me ler precisa ter fôlego. Sou um texto imenso. Sou reticências, capítulos e fascículos. Para me ler, tem que me tocar. Sou escrita em braile. Sou toda sentidos. Sou toda eu.‬

[...]To be happy in a realistic way is to do the possible and accept the improbable. Do exercises without craving walkways, work without craving for stardom, love without craving the eternal.

Look at the clock: Time to wake up.

It is important to think in the extreme, to search within what mobilizes, instigates and leads, but without demanding inhumanity. Life is not a game where only those who test their limits take the prize.

Let's not be naive victims of such competitiveness. If the goal is too high, reduce it. If you do not agree with the rules, quit. Invent your own game.

Do whatever it takes to be happy. But do not forget that happiness is a simple feeling, you can find it and let it go away by not realizing its simplicity. It conveys peace and not strong feelings, which torments us and causes restlessness in our hearts. This can be joy, passion, enthusiasm, but not happiness. — Mario Quintana.
